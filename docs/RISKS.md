# Risks

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|
| Revenue arrives too late | Weak business viability score | Sell paid pilot packages by 2026-06-15 and track monthly revenue from first customer. |
| Users are not independent | Revenue may be discounted | Separate arms-length revenue from related-party revenue and prioritize new third-party customers. |
| Product feels like a submission helper, not a business | Weak category impact | Position as founder operations desk with recurring paid workflows, not one-off hackathon tooling. |
| AI usage is shallow | Weak AI-native operations score | Put Gemini in planning, scoring, critique, evidence synthesis, and continuous decision loops. |
| Google Cloud proof is thin | Eligibility risk | Deploy demo/API to Google Cloud and capture logs/usage receipts early. |
| Automation touches external forms unreliably | Submission risk | Use field maps, draft-save runs, screenshots, and manual review receipts before final submission. |
| Private financial/user data leaks | Trust and eligibility risk | Keep private evidence out of public repo and publish only redacted hashes/summaries. |
| Existing local code predates contest | New-project clarity risk | Disclose reused frameworks and show newly created ContestOps product code after 2026-05-19. |
| Video exceeds 3 minutes | Judging loss | Rehearse to 2:45 and keep a captioned transcript. |
| Repo missing judge access | Testing risk | Public repo or private share to `testing@devpost.com` and `judging@hacker.fund`. |
| Form requirements change | Late rework | Re-run Devpost rule/form scrape weekly and before final submit. |
| Single long file growth | Technical debt | Split CLI, domain, Gemini, evidence, and UI components before any file reaches 600 lines. |
