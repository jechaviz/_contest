# Competitive Strategy

Version: `v0.4.0`.

Goal: beat stronger-looking AI startups by making ContestOps AI the clearest
example of a business operated by AI, not just a product that contains AI.

## Likely Competitor Archetypes

| Archetype | Why they score | Weak point | Counter |
|---|---|---|---|
| Vertical AI startup with revenue | Clear buyer, clean demo, credible business | AI may be a feature, not operating model | Show Gemini and agents running discovery, package generation, sales ops, evidence, and submissions. |
| Generic agent platform | Broad ambition and technical polish | Hard to prove focused buyer and revenue | Lead with paid pilot packages and founder operations niche. |
| Grant/proposal writer | Easy workflow and obvious value | Mostly writing; little execution evidence | Show end-to-end rules, build backlog, evidence ledger, form automation, and receipts. |
| Devtool/coding agent | Judges understand developer productivity | Crowded and less directly job-creation aligned | Show opportunity access for nontechnical founders and SMB operators. |
| CRM/workflow automation clone | Familiar SaaS motion | Weak AI-native story | Make decisions, receipts, and automated submissions the center of the demo. |

## 10x Positioning

Bad positioning: "AI tool that helps apply to contests."

Winning positioning: "AI-native founder operations system that creates
economic opportunity by finding, packaging, proving, and submitting high-value
business opportunities with audit-ready evidence."

## Proof Wedge

The product should prove four things in one run:

1. Opportunity source becomes structured rules.
2. Gemini turns rules into a prioritized execution plan.
3. Agents produce artifacts, evidence, and external-form payloads.
4. The same workflow sells as a paid pilot with customer receipts.

## Demo Priorities

- Open with the operations console, not a landing page.
- Show official rules scrape and generated checklist.
- Show judge scorecard and competitor battlecard.
- Show Devpost payload and WAIBAv trace.
- Show paid pilot offer and revenue ledger slots.
- Show Cloud Run API and Gemini receipt path.

## What Must Be True By `v1.0.0`

- Three arms-length paid pilots are closed or at least invoiced.
- Three users have feedback records and quote permissions.
- One Cloud Run URL is public and logged.
- One env-backed Gemini receipt exists for each demo package.
- One redacted customer case study is public.
- Demo video includes a before/after proof, not only UI navigation.
