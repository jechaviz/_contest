# Competitor Battleplan

Version: `v0.5.0`.

## Primary Threats

| Threat | Why It Can Win | Counter |
|---|---|---|
| Vertical AI startup with revenue | Judges can understand buyer, price, demo, and traction quickly. | Run the proof sprint to show 3 pilots, receipts, and founder outcomes. |
| Generic agent platform | Broad automation demo can look technically stronger. | Show domain-specific rules, evidence boundaries, Devpost payloads, and external form receipts. |
| Proposal-writing assistant | Simpler story and obvious before/after writing gain. | Show full opportunity operations: source, plan, build, evidence, automation, submission. |
| AI devtool startup | Strong technical audience fit and clear productivity value. | Show job creation for founders and SMB operators, not only developer productivity. |
| Services agency | Human trust and polished deliverables. | Productize repeatable receipts, automated QA, and faster turnaround. |

## Judge Hooks

- Business: `3 pilots / 5 calls / 20 prospects` is the traction scoreboard.
- Product: one opportunity becomes rules, plan, evidence, payload, and draft.
- AI: Gemini is a deployed planning and critique layer, not a hidden writer.
- Cloud: Cloud Run and Cloud Logging receipts show a running product.
- Automation: WAIBAv traces show draft preparation with a final-submit gate.
- Impact: more founders can access contests, grants, RFPs, and accelerators.

## Rebuttal Lines

- Against "just a proposal writer": ContestOps AI does not stop at text; it
  produces evidence, receipts, payloads, and submission automation.
- Against "just an agent demo": the workflow is scoped to a buyer, a category,
  and a judge-verifiable proof sprint.
- Against "no revenue yet": the sales kit and ledger are live, and the sprint
  has exact metrics for converting pilots into evidence.
- Against "too much operations": the operations are the product and the
  submission demonstrates the business operating itself.
