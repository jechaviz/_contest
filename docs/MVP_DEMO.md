# Demo MVP

Product name: ContestOps AI.

Primary category: Entrepreneurship & Job Creation.

Thesis: founders and small teams leave money and traction on the table because
opportunity discovery, rule interpretation, evidence collection, demo building,
and submission operations are fragmented. ContestOps AI runs those workflows as
AI-native operations, with Gemini planning and Google Cloud deployment receipts.

## MVP Flow

1. Intake: user adds a contest, grant, RFP, or startup application URL.
2. Ingest: VImport extracts rules, deadlines, requirements, and form fields.
3. Plan: Gemini generates a scoped execution plan and scoring hypotheses.
4. Build: V modules create checklists, evidence manifests, runbooks, and
   submission drafts.
5. Execute: WAIBAv playbooks prepare external forms, capture screenshots, and
   collect run receipts.
6. Operate: daily jobs update opportunity status, customer proof, revenue
   ledgers, and production evidence.
7. Prove: the proof sprint tracks buyer, user, Cloud Run, Gemini, and draft
   receipts against judge-visible targets.
8. Submit: the app maps the package to Devpost fields and captures confirmation.
